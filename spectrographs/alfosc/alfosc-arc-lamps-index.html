ALFOSC Arc Lamps

The arc lamps currently available for ALFOSC

Spectral lamps from Osram: Ne, He, Cs, Zn (spare He and Ne)
Hollow cathode lamps from Cathodeon: Nb, Cu, Th (spare Nb)

The Osram Cs and Zn lamps use Ar as a filler gas. The Cathodeon Nb and Th lamps also have Ar as a filler gas, while the Cu lamp has Ne as a filler. All this means that the Cs, Zn, Nb and Th lamps all give Ar lines as well as lines from the appropriate element while the Cu lamp gives Ne lines as well as Cu lines.

Beware! the lines from Nb and Cu are all faint and generally require a integration of up to 20 minutes to detect them!

Wavelength calibration.
NB: These arc maps give no indication of exposure times needed!!
